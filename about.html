about Charity

A nation's real wealth is its educated and cultured people. Annapoorna Charitable Trust, established in the year 2004, embarked on its major goal of creating a home for the underprivileged children of our society by providing them with a nice parental home and making them responsible citizens of the nation. To fulfill this, "BALYA" a free home for children was started in the year 2005 with two children Phalguni and Vaisakhi. Managing trustee and founder, Mrs. Annapoorna brings with her years of experience at a school that catered to physically and mentally disabled children prior to starting Balya. Her deep concern and compassion to help the deprived culminated in forming the Trust with like minded people and she continues to run and contribute to the cause of charity with the same zeal and compassion.
