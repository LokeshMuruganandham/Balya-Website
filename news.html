NEWS

Annapoorna Charitable Trust is making great strides in expanding its impact. Balya Vidya Mandir is now set to accommodate higher secondary education (9th-12th grade), ensuring that more students can continue their education within our facilities. In addition, we have introduced new vocational training programs in IT, digital marketing, and entrepreneurship, providing children with valuable skills for better career opportunities. We are proud to share some of our recent success stories. One of our talented students, Aditya, had the incredible opportunity to represent India in an Art & Culture Showcase in London and Wales. Moreover, 10 students from our skill development programs have secured placements in reputed companies like Infosys and IBO, marking a significant step toward self-sufficiency. With growing support from donors and well-wishers, we continue to expand our facilities and educational programs, ensuring that every child has a brighter future. Stay tuned for more updates on our journey of transformation and empowerment!
