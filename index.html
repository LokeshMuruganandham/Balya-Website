About Charity

A nation's real wealth is its educated and cultured people. Annapoorna Charitable Trust, established in the year 2004, embarked on its major goal of creating a home for the underprivileged children of our society by providing them with a nice parental home and making them responsible citizens of the nation. To fulfill this, "BALYA" a free home for children was started in the year 2005 with two children Phalguni and Vaisakhi.

FUTURE CAUSE

At Annapoorna Charitable Trust, we are constantly striving to expand our reach and impact. Our future cause focuses on enhancing education, infrastructure, and skill development to empower more underprivileged children.

Expansion of BALYA Vidya Mandir

  • Build more classrooms to accommodate higher secondary education (9th-12th grade) within our premises.
  • Upgrade learning resources, including smart classrooms, libraries, and science labs.

Vocational Training & Career Development

  • Introduce new skill-based training programs in IT, digital marketing, and entrepreneurship.
  • Partner with organizations to secure job placements and scholarships for our students.

Better Living Facilities for Children

  • Expand BALYA Free Homes to provide better dormitories, recreational areas, and healthcare support.
  • Improve access to mental health counseling and wellness programs.
